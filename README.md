# Invoice Generator

!! This is just a project that didn't exist, and that I wanted to exist. It was created with Claude rather than being hand-written. It does not represent my own work, and is only made public to benefit others, not to be used as part of my portfolio.

## About this project

Create a professional invoice in your browser and download it as a PDF for free.

**No account. No sign-up. No ads. No limits. No watermarks.**
Unfortunately that's not the default anymore.

**[Try it for yourself](https://larsfl.github.io/Invoice-Generator/)** or clone, self-host, etc.

## What it does

- Fill in your company details, your client, and what you're billing for.
- See a live preview of the finished invoice as you type.
- Download a clean, print-ready PDF, not just an image.

## Features

- 🇳🇱 / 🇬🇧 **Dutch & English** — choose the language of the app and of the invoice
  separately (e.g. a Dutch company billing an English client). More languages to be added.
- 💶 **Choose your currency**, formatted correctly for the chosen language.
- 🧾 **VAT / BTW** per line with common rates, multiple rates on one invoice, and a
  reverse-charge ("BTW verlegd") option.
- 🖼️ **Your logo** on the invoice.
- 🌓 **Light & dark mode**, following your system by default.

## Your privacy

Everything you enter stays in your browser's local storage — nothing is uploaded,
tracked, or shared. There is no server. If you want a backup or to move your details
to another device, use **Export** to save a JSON file of your preferences and **Import** to load it again.

Because data lives in your browser, clearing your browser data will also clear your
saved details. Keep an exported JSON file if you want to be safe.

## A note on tax

This tool helps you lay out an invoice; it does **not** give tax or legal advice.
Please check that your invoices meet the rules that apply to you and your country.
